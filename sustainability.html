Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Airbus, sustainability is at the core of our mission. We aim to create a cleaner, more efficient future for aviation. Our initiatives focus on reducing emissions, improving fuel efficiency, and developing new technologies that lead the industry toward sustainable aviation.

Key Areas of Focus

  • Hydrogen-Powered Flight: Airbus is working on hydrogen propulsion technology to power commercial aircraft by 2035.
  • Sustainable Aviation Fuels (SAF): Airbus continues to innovate with SAF, which can reduce the carbon footprint of aviation by up to 80%.
  • Eco-Design: Our design process incorporates sustainability from the beginning, ensuring that every aircraft is efficient, lightweight, and environmentally friendly.
  • Zero Emission Aircraft: Airbus is committed to bringing the world's first zero-emission commercial aircraft to the market by 2035.

Our Goals

We have set ambitious goals to reduce our environmental impact, including:

  • Reducing CO2 emissions by 50% by 2050
  • Increasing the use of sustainable materials in aircraft production
  • Implementing circular economy principles throughout the production process
